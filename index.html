About

Below are some of the details about buddo ss

Buddo S.S is a mixed, boarding secondary school for O’ and A’ Level, with excellent boarding facilities. Buddo S.S is fully registered by the Ministry of Education and sports since 4th February 1991 under a UNEB centre number U0763. The school is interdenominational. Students attend prayers according to their religious affiliation declared by their parents on admission. E.g. Protestants have a chaplain, Catholics have a chaplain, Muslims attend Juma prayers every Friday and Adventists are also catered for as well. A high quality education is one of the most important things that we can provide a child. Exposure to a variety of topics, people and adventures all contribute to a fulfilling school experience like Buddo S.S.
